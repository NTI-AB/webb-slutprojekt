About Royale

Founded in 2015 by acclaimed Chef Marie Laurent, Royale brings refined European dining to San Francisco with a distinctly Californian approach.

"We believe dining is an art form that engages all the senses, and we strive to create memorable experiences that honor both tradition and innovation."

The Wine Bar

An exquisite collection curated by our Master Sommelier

Our intimate wine bar features a carefully curated selection of over 200 wines from renowned regions around the world. From rare vintage champagnes to exceptional local discoveries, our collection offers something for both connoisseurs and casual enthusiasts.

Join us for wine flights, expert-led tastings, or simply unwind with a perfectly paired glass and small plate. Our knowledgeable sommeliers are always available to guide you through our extensive cellar and help discover new favorites.

The Bakery

Artisanal pastries and breads crafted daily

Our in-house bakery creates exceptional artisanal breads and pastries using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. Led by renowned pastry chef Marie Laurent, our team begins work before dawn to ensure everything is fresh for your morning visit.

From flaky croissants and pain au chocolat to rustic sourdough loaves and seasonal fruit tarts, each item is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. Pair your selection with our specialty coffee or take home a box of treats to enjoy later.
